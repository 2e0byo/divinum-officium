[Oratio]
May the intercession of the Blessed Abbot Sabbas, commend us, we beseech You, O Lord, so that what we do not deserve by any merits of ours we may obtain by his patronage.
$Per Dominum

[Secreta1]
In thy kindness, O Lord, receive the gifts which we have joyfully offered thee, and grant through the intercession of blessed Sabbas, that thy Church may rejoice in soundness of faith, and ever be gladdened by days of peacefulness. 
$Per Dominum

[Postcommunio]
Multiply, we beseech thee, O Lord, in thy Church the spirit of grace which tho hast bestowed, that by intercession of blessed Sabbas, the obedience of the flock shall not fail the shepherd, nor the care of the shepherd be wanting to the flock.
$Per Dominum
